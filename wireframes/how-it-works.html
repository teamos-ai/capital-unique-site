Expectations

What happens when you engage with us

Capital Unique moves with purpose and transparency. Here is what you can expect from the moment you reach out.

  • Timeline

    From enquiry to capital deployment typically takes one to four weeks, depending on scenario complexity.

  • What we need

    Business background, scenario details, and supporting documentation allow us to assess properly.

  • How we decide

    Each scenario receives structured, experienced judgement from our team, not algorithmic scoring.

  • When it's not a fit

    We provide clarity quickly if a scenario cannot proceed, without wasting your time.

Fit

This is built for specific scenarios

Capital Unique serves borrowers with complex needs and structured ambitions. This is not for everyone, and that is intentional.

  • Complex capital needs

    Scenarios traditional banks decline or cannot accommodate quickly.

  • Time-sensitive situations

    Business growth, development, or investment opportunities with real timing constraints.

  • Structured solutions

    Borrowers seeking flexible terms aligned with their specific business or project structure.

  • Business and commercial focus

    We serve businesses, developers, and investors, not consumer or retail lending.
