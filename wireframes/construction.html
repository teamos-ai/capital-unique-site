Construction capital is staged, not fixed

Unlike traditional lending, construction capital moves with your project. Funds release in draws aligned to completion milestones, cost verification, and delivery risk—not approval formulas or credit scores alone. This is how active builds actually get funded.

Assessment

How we assess construction capital

We don't use formulas. We assess reality. The build. The timing. The risk. This is what guides every decision.

  • Editorial landscape photo — site programme/Gantt sheet, architect's plans on a builder's bench, or considered detail of build planning. Real photography.

    Project

    We structure around your build schedule. The program comes first. Price comes later.

  • Editorial landscape photo — quantity surveyor on site, signed progress claim, or scaffolded floor mid-stage. Real photography.

    Draws

    Capital releases follow progress. Milestones verified. Costs checked. Money moves when work is done.

  • Editorial landscape photo — principal walking site with builder, hard-hat conversation in considered light. Real photography, no stock-finance imagery.

    Risk

    Risk is assessed before capital is committed. Delivery risk. Market risk. Execution risk. No surprises mid-build.

  • Editorial landscape photo — experienced operator at a desk with build documents, or principal in conversation with developer. Real photography.

    Judgement

    Decisions are made by people. Not models. Not algorithms. Construction experience matters.

Process

How construction capital moves

No unnecessary steps. No wasted time.

  1. 01 — Listen

    We listen and understand your operation, seasonal patterns, asset position, and what capital needs to achieve.

  2. 02 — Assess

    We evaluate how capital can work within your farm structure, cashflow timing, and any regulatory or operational constraints.

  3. 03 — Confirm

    Once structure is clear, we confirm how the capital will be accessed, repaid, and what protections are in place.

  4. 04 — Move

    Capital is deployed when terms are agreed and documentation is complete, with no unnecessary delays.
