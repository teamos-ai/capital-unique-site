II. Approach  ·  Built for those who think differently Section 02 / 06

Capital Unique exists where traditional banking ends. We understand complex commercial deals, unconventional scenarios, and the investors who fund them — and we work with people who would rather have a conversation than fill in another form. Where the bank sees a deviation from policy, we see the underlying logic of the deal.

Our work sits between two audiences who rarely sit at the same table. On one side, borrowers with capable businesses, real assets, and timelines that don't bend to credit-committee calendars. On the other, private investors and family offices looking for governed exposure, not a brochure of products. Most of what we do is to make those two needs meet, intelligibly, on terms that hold up under scrutiny.

We move quietly, think carefully, and deliver with precision. Decisions are made by the people who structure them. Conversations are short. Documentation is plain. The capital, when it arrives, is the easy part.

The discipline is consistent across mandates. Same team. Same standard. Same patience for the complicated case the bank wouldn't read past the first page. The deal sets the cadence — not the calendar.

III. The Process  ·  How funding works Section 03 / 06

We start by understanding your objectives and assessing the opportunity with care. From there, we structure the right solution and guide the process through to funding with clarity at every stage. The approach is deliberate, transparent, and designed to move forward with confidence.

  1. 01.

    Understand the deal

    We discuss your business objectives, timing, and structure so we can understand what you're trying to achieve. The first conversation is short and specific — what the capital is for, when it needs to be in place, and what's already moving.

    No forms. No prequalification theatre. Just enough to know whether we're the right hands for the work.

  2. 02.

    Assess options

    We review the information and identify the most suitable funding approach based on your needs, your timeline, and the underlying asset or cash flow. Where there's a cleaner structure, we'll say so. Where the conventional path is best, we'll say that too.

    Most deals reveal their right structure within a few hours of careful reading.

  3. 03.

    Confirm terms

    We clearly explain the proposed terms, costs, and conditions before anything moves forward. The numbers are written plainly. The risks are named, not hidden in the small print. Nothing in the documentation is intended to surprise you later.

    Confirm in writing. Move when you're ready.

  4. 04.

    Deploy funding

    Once agreed, we manage the process through to funding and remain involved as the capital is deployed. The relationship doesn't end at settlement — most of our work begins there, in the quiet stretch between the term sheet and the result the deal was built for.

    Same team, same standard, all the way through.
