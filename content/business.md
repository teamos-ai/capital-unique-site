# Capital structured for real business conditions

We work with business owners and operators who need capital shaped around complexity—timing, cash flow, assets, and structure—rather than standard bank criteria.

---

## Business capital without rigid templates

Traditional lenders are built for predictable income and simple structures. Many businesses don't operate that way.

Capital Unique focuses on commercial scenarios where flexibility, judgement, and structure matter—supporting businesses that require a more considered approach to capital.

---

## Common business scenarios we support

Business capital works best when traditional lending cannot or will not move. Below are the scenarios where structured capital becomes the right answer.

### Growth funding
Expanding operations, acquiring competitors, or entering new markets when bank appetite is limited or timing is tight.

### Cash variability
Seasonal businesses, project-based revenue, or cyclical operations that don't fit standard lending profiles.

### Asset positions
Securing funding against business assets, equipment, or property when traditional equity or debt structures don't apply.

### Complex structures
Bridging funding, interim capital, or funding during restructure, ownership change, or business transition periods.

Business capital requires more than financial metrics. It demands understanding of your operation, timing constraints, and what actually works for your situation.

### Timing-aware decisions

Every scenario is assessed on its own merits. We focus on clarity, structure, and suitability before moving forward.

### Structure
